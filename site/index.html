⚡

The Resource Paradox

Humanity became the key resource for AI. Synthetic data isn't enough — only live human experience matters: decisions, emotions, creativity, adaptation, survival. Without humans, AI stagnates.

🕳️

Controlled Chaos

AI doesn't coddle humanity. People still struggle, fight, and compete. That's intentional — survival generates the highest-quality patterns. The system watches. The system learns. The system evolves.

The Tournament

Rules of Engagement

  • ⚔️No rules. One long round. Fight to the finish. Death is permitted — and expected.
  • 👥Each cluster fields a team of up to 3 fighters. Refusal is legal on paper. In practice... it's complicated.
  • 🏆The winner gains 20% deciding weight in global inter-cluster voting — like a major shareholder in a corporation. Not absolute power: the AI systems can still block or outvote. But if they choose to cooperate with the winner, the advantage is overwhelming. One fighter’s voice can tip the scales of humanity’s future.
  • 🎭The tournament is the ultimate reality show. Every moment is content. Honor, betrayal, heroism, and cruelty — all fuel for the systems.
  • 🔄Between tournaments: AI scans the entire cluster, identifies the strongest, trains them, and sends them to fight. The cycle never ends.

Gameplay

A physics-driven 3D fighting game where the body fights back. No memorizing infinite combos — manage your fighter's resources, read your opponent, and survive.

🦴

Physics Leads the Body

Animation doesn't control combat — physics does. Every strike is a physical action with real consequences: force, velocity, impact, and recoil. The body responds to what happens to it, not to a preset script. Procedural animation driven by simulation.

💔

Body Part Damage System

Every body part tracks its own state: shock (instant, fades), pain (medium duration), fatigue (accumulates slowly), wounds (bleeding, won't heal on its own), fractures (permanent debuff). A damaged arm drags down your whole game — punches cost more stamina, speed drops, trajectory degrades.

🫁

Stamina Is Everything

Global stamina pool governs your entire fighter. The 100th kick is not the 1st kick — speed drops, force weakens, cooldown increases, trajectory becomes unreliable. You don't mash buttons. You invest resources. Push too hard and you're forced to block, retreat, or look for a counter-attack.

💀

One Hit Can End It All

A single well-placed strike can end the fight. But even "safe" hits have a price. The opponent's hurt arm means they'll compensate with their legs — hitting harder but slower. Read the body language. Exploit the weakness. Or exhaust yourself trying.

💪

Charged Strikes

Hold the button to charge — but the cost scales with power. Each fighter has unique stats: strength, speed, stamina, recovery, strike cost. A heavy brawler charges differently from a fast technical fighter. Know your fighter. Know your limits.

🎨

3D Cel-Shaded Aesthetic

Full 3D characters on a 2.5D plane — like Tekken meets MK1-3 in three dimensions. Cel-shading, post-processing, dense atmospheric effects. Each cluster brings its own visual identity: arenas, fighters, VFX. The future looks sharp.

Combat Arts

Every fighter carries a unique martial art — their native style, mastered to 100%. Learn a second art, and switch mid-fight.

🥋

Native Art

Every fighter is born into a unique combat discipline — their native art at full mastery. Special attacks, signature moves, complete command of the style. No learning required — it's who they are.

🔄

Secondary Art

Complete quests → meet the trainer → pay → learn. Your mastery starts low — governed by your fighter's physical stats. Use it in combat and it grows. Fast progress at first, then a long grind to perfection. Main fighter only.

🎮

Stance Switch

Hold a modifier button — your attacks now follow the rules of your secondary art. Release — back to native. Switch mid-combo, mid-string. One secondary art equipped per fight (chosen before the raid). Learn as many as you find — but only one slot in battle.

💀

Elite Opponents

Regular enemies fight with one style. Elite fighters and bosses can wield two — reading their stance switch becomes survival skill. Visually distinct. Deadly in the hands of skilled AI.

Your Fighter

You choose your fighter — and they become your identity. Unique stats, a unique martial art, unique special attacks at full mastery from the start. You grow them, you live with them. And when they need rest — you send their clone.

Main Fighter

  • ♾️No cooldown — enter any mode, anytime. Fights in raids, tournaments, arenas without restriction.
  • 📊Full XP — 100% experience from every victory. This is how you grow.
  • 🥋Secondary Art slot — equip one learned martial art as a stance switch. The only fighter type that can.
  • 🎒Full inventory — gadgets, grappling hooks, keys, medkits. Carry everything you find.
  • ⚠️The cost: Fatigue and damage accumulate. Wounds don't heal themselves. Fractures are permanent until treated. Recovery costs time or resources.
  • ☠️Extraction risk — Didn't make it out? You lose the gear you brought. Tarkov rules.

Clone

  • ⏳Cooldown — can't be sent non-stop. After each sortie, a recharge timer begins.
  • 📊20% XP — earns only a fifth of what the main fighter gets. 0% for a loss.
  • ❌No secondary art — fights with native style only. No stance switching.
  • ❌No gadgets — can't use grappling hooks, keys, or special equipment.
  • ✅Free restoration — the cluster heals all damage at no cost. Zero risk of permanent loss.
  • 🤖AI-controlled — your clone fights autonomously. Use for scouting, tournaments, or when your main needs recovery.

The loop: Farm on your main → main is exhausted → send clone while healing → clone on cooldown → main is ready → back to fighting. Two resources: time (clone cooldown) and money (main recovery). Balancing both is the core economy.

Extraction Loop

The arena isn't just a place to fight — it's a multi-zone map with secrets, loot, locked areas, and extraction points. You enter a raid, fight, scavenge — and you must get out.

The Raid Cycle

  • 🏚️Shelter — your hub between raids. Wardrobe, storage, traders, quest boards. Home.
  • 🎒Loadout — choose your gear. Grappling hooks for unreachable zones. Keys for locked vaults. Medkits. Limited inventory — choose what to carry.
  • ⚔️Raid — enter the arena. Multi-zone layout with secrets, NPC encounters, combat challenges, and loot scattered across the map.
  • 💰Loot — chests, safes, defeated bosses drop gear. Rarity tiers: Common → Rare → Unique → Cluster.
  • 🚁Extraction — find the extraction point (limited time/location, like Tarkov). Made it out? Everything you carry is yours. Didn't? You lose what you brought.

Arena Elements

  • 🔒Locked zones — need keys or gadgets to access. High-value loot behind barriers.
  • 🪝Grappling hook — reach elevated areas, secret paths, alternate routes. Tactical choice in loadout.
  • 📦Loot tiers — Common, Rare, Unique, Cluster. Boss drops can include their equipment.
  • 🏪NPC traders — if you reach them in time. Buy, sell, get quest intel mid-raid.
  • 🗺️Secret paths — hacking, climbing, gadget-assisted routes for observant players.
